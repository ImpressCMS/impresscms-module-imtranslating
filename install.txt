How to install Imtranslating
=============================

Imtranslating is installed as a regular XOOPS module, which means you should copy the complete /imtranslating folder into the /modules directory of your website. Then, log in to your site as administrator, go to System Admin > Modules, look for the Imtranslating icon in the list of uninstalled modules and click in the install icon. Follow the directions in the screen and you'll be ready to go.
